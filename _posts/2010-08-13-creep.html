So, I’ve got a young gentleman from France CouchSurfing with me. He told me he’d make us creeps. I didn’t think you could do that to another person. Then I realized he was saying crêpes. Which I tend to pronounce as creyps. Benjamin making us crêpes rather than creeps, to me, was a very good thing. Best of all, there were leftovers, to which I added bacon (of course), scrambled eggs, and chèvre (goat cheese). I would have posted a picture, but I’ve gone and eaten it all.
