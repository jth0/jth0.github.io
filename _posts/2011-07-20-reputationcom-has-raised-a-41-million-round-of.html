Reputation.com has raised a $41 million round of funding, as the online privacy start-up aims to expand its business with new offerings. … Fertik said Reputation.com decided to raise more money because it is planning a big push into a new line of business. To date, the company has offered several services that help consumers manage their privacy, information and reputation online. Now Reputation.com has also built a dataset business–essentially, the start-up has collected data on individuals and layered that information with its own technologies–which allows the company to come up with assessments on people, among other things.
—Sorry, Reputation.com, but if you’re making your business the collection of information to profile individuals, you are no longer a “privacy” start-up.
