I have a few friends with LJ blogs. I tried to keep one updated myself. That didn’t work so well, either because I didn’t know what to write, or didn’t think to share something funny, etc. I’m thinking tumblr’s format might allow me to post a little more. And if nothing else, I can use it to aggregate feeds of other sites where some content actually generated by me can be found.

If I did new year’s resolutions, I might make one stating “I will keep updating my tumblr web-corner.” But I don’t do resolutions, and I probably will have to try hard to keep updating this site. Actually, I do one resolution, but it’s the same each year: make it through another year. Conveniently, new year’s resolutions for me are also birthday resolutions.

Happy John’s Birthday Eve!
