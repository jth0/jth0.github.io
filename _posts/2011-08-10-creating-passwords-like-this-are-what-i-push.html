Creating passwords like this are what I push people towards. Unfortunately, many “secure” systems like banks–ha!–don’t allow long passwords.

Adding punctuation still helps, as does repeating characters to break at least one word from being found in a dictionary. Think “purple monkkey dishwasher”. Either way, passwords like this are easier to recall and easier to type.
