…earlier this decade we had a freak rain storm that produced a lot of hail in [Southern California]. The quantity and ferocity allowed for the frozen precipitation to collect on the ground and if you looked at it from the SkyChopper 5 Live-Feed, it looked like snow. This was enough for the inner-city youth to lie on the ground in front a local 7-11 to make what the reporter lady called “snow angels”. I called it “sadness”.
