I do not thank you for turning me onto this game. I installed it on the way down stairs, missed several elevators and then found myself standing in the middle of the hallway, it was a very tense situation.
—

Ted found Flight Control

I had a very similar situation two weeks ago. A coworker handed me his iPhone and walked away. I looked at it as two planes crashed in mid-air, then I tried again. A half-hour later, my coworker had to physically remove his iPhone from my hands. So I took my iPhone out of my pocket, bought Flight Control and played for another half-hour.

I think I remember why I don’t play games.
