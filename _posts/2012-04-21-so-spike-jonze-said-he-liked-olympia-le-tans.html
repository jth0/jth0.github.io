Spike Jonze: Mourir Auprès de Toi on Nowness.com.


So Spike Jonze said he liked Olympia Le-Tan’s embroidered designs and asked for an embroidered cover of “The Catcher In the Rye”. She capitulated on the condition that Spike make her a film. And so we are left with “Mourir Auprès de Toi” which is an adorable stop-motion film considering what may happen in a cozy book shop after dark. It’s title translates to “To Die By Your Side”. J'adore ça.
