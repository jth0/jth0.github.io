It took several rounds of rewrites to get the simple tag cloud script right, and this made me very skittish about touching any other parts of the code over the next few days, even when the fixes were easy and obvious. The part of my brain that knew what to do no longer seemed to be connected directly to my hands.
—Pinboard got crushed last December, two weeks after I signed up. Very interesting walkthrough of a good Bad Day and the effects felt when two or three days into an IT crisis.
